🔗 CRM Integration Guide
1
Configure Your CRM Webhook
Open Settings (⚙️) and add your CRM webhook URL. LeadQualifier supports HubSpot and Salesforce webhooks.
2
HubSpot Webhook Setup
Create a workflow in HubSpot that triggers on "Custom" event. Use this endpoint format:
https://api.hsforms.com/submissions/v3/integration/submit/YOUR_PORTAL_ID/YOUR_FORM_GUID
3
Salesforce Webhook Setup
Create a REST API endpoint in Salesforce or use a middleware like Zapier:
https://your-instance.my.salesforce.com/services/apexrest/LeadQualifier
4
Send Scores to CRM
After scoring a lead, click "Send to CRM" button. The score, tier, and all signals will be sent to your configured webhook.
