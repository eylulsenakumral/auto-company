### Challenges Encountered

#### Challenge 1: Multi-Location Companies
**Problem**: Several companies operate multiple plants across Turkey
**Examples**:
- Hattat: HQ in Çerkezköy, not Bursa
- Toyotetsu: Primary in Kocaeli (TOSB), not Bursa
- Faurecia: Plants in Kocaeli (AKOSB) and Bursa (Orhangazi)

**Solution**: Used Bursa-specific plant contacts where available, marked others as "not Bursa"

#### Challenge 2: Parent vs. Local Entity
**Problem**: Some companies listed under parent/group names
**Examples**:
- BEM Bosch: Part of Bosch Sanayi A.Ş.
- Magna Otomotiv: Subsidiary of Magna International
- FAW: Chinese HQ, no verified Bursa plant

**Solution**: Used parent company contacts, marked for verification

#### Challenge 3: Generic Email Addresses
**Problem**: Most companies use generic emails (info@, contact@, satis@)
**Impact**: Limited direct EHS coordinator contacts
**Solution**: Will need phone calls to request EHS coordinator names

#### Challenge 4: Referral-Only Data
**Problem**: Some companies only listed in OSB management directories
**Examples**: CONTITECH, BOY TEKNİK, BURÇAK METAL
**Solution**: Marked as "Referral" - need OSB directory lookup or direct inquiry
