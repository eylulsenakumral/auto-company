## A/B Test Design

### Variant A (Direct Approach) - Companies 1-3
**Focus:** Direct value proposition, immediate offer

**Subject:** Bursa Otomotiv — İş Güvenliği Benchmark Raporu

**Key Elements:**
- "Analiz eden bir sistem kurduk" (We built a system)
- "Anonim benchmark" (Anonymous benchmark)
- "Katılım ücretsiz" (Free participation)
- "5 dakikalık demo" (5-minute demo)

**Hypothesis:** Direct approach works better for Tier-2 companies who want concrete tools

### Variant B (Comparative Approach) - Companies 4-7
**Focus:** Social proof, competitive positioning

**Subject:** Bursa Otomotiv — İSG Uzmanları Arasında Benchmark Raporu

**Key Elements:**
- "Analiz ediyoruz" (We are analyzing)
- "Diğer fabrikalarla kıyasla" (Compare with other factories)
- "Nerede olduğunuzu görün" (See where you stand)
- "İlginiz var mı?" (Are you interested?)

**Hypothesis:** Competitive angle works better for Tier-1 companies who care about relative performance
