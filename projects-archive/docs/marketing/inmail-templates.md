## A/B Test Hypothesis

### What We're Testing

**Primary Hypothesis:** Story-driven emotional messaging (Variant A) will outperform value-driven rational messaging (Variant B) for EHS coordinators in Bursa automotive suppliers.

**Why:**

1. **Tribes Principle:** EHS coordinators identify as "protectors" — their identity is wrapped up in preventing failures, not optimizing costs. Stories about failures trigger their defensive psychology.

2. **Permission Principle:** Variant A earns attention by sharing a relatable war story ("this happened to a peer"). Variant B assumes permission to lecture about efficiency — most EHS folks already know they're under-auditing.

3. **Purple Cow Factor:** Variant A positions NextVision as a "safety culture engine" (remarkable). Variant B positions it as "audit cost reduction tool" (commodity).

**Secondary Hypothesis:** Subject Line A2 ("Lost a €50K order...") will have higher open rate than A1 because specific loss numbers trigger loss aversion bias.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Variant A Prediction | Variant B Prediction |
|--------|--------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Reply Rate | >20% | 25-30% | 15-20% |
| Open Rate | >40% | 45-50% (A2 > A1) | 40-45% |
| Call Booking | >10% | 12-15% | 8-12% |
| Forward Rate | >5% | 8-10% (story worth sharing) | 3-5% |

### Decision Rules

**If Variant A wins (reply rate ≥25%):**
→ Roll out Variant A to all 50 prospects
→ A/B test within A: Subject line A1 vs A2

**If Variant B wins (reply rate ≥25%):**
→ Roll out Variant B to all 50 prospects
→ Hypothesis failed: EHS coordinators are more rational than tribal → Adjust persona

**If both <15% reply rate:**
→ Pivot to textile sector (Denizli dyeing/finishing)
→ New message: "Stop chemical exposure fines. Auto-detect PPE gaps in dye stations."

## Forward Worthiness (Godin's "Idea Diffusion")

**Variant A is designed to be worth forwarding because:**
→ It's a story, not a pitch (people share stories)
→ It has a villain (EU auditor) and hero (tech solution)
→ It creates "I should tell my boss about this" moment

**Variant B is less forwardable because:**
→ It's a cost analysis (boring to share)
→ No narrative tension (just numbers)
→ "I'll deal with this later" energy

**Virality trigger:** If Variant A gets 1 forward per 20 sends, it's a Purple Cow.
