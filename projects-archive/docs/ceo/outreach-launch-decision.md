# CEO Decision Memo: Staged Launch Strategy

**Date**: 2026-06-03
**Decision**: Option B — Staged Launch (7-company cohort + parallel research)
**Decision Maker**: CEO-Bezos
**Decision Speed**: 70% information, action > perfection
**Risk Level**: LOW — Reversible two-way door

## Decision Framework

### 1. Customer Obsession (Working Backwards)

**Question**: Does the pilot customer care if we contact 7 companies today or 43 companies tomorrow?

**Answer**: The customer cares about **quality of outreach**, not quantity. But they also care about **momentum**.

A staged launch lets us:
- Start learning immediately (real feedback loop)
- Maintain quality on every interaction
- Show progress to stakeholders

**Winner**: Staged launch balances both.

### 2. Two-Way Door Decision

This is **completely reversible**. If the 7-company cohort performs poorly, we stop, research more, and re-launch. If it performs well, we scale research in parallel while executing.

**Key insight**: We're not committing to a bad dataset. We're committing to a **learning loop**.

### 3. Flywheel Effect Analysis

```
Better data → More successful outreach → More meetings → Better learning → Better targeting
```

**Option A breaks the flywheel** (stops everything for research).
**Option B keeps the flywheel spinning** (execute + research in parallel).
**Option C partially spins** (tiered approach).

**Winner**: Option B maximizes flywheel velocity.

### 4. Regret Minimization Framework

At 80 years old, would I regret:
- **Waiting 1 day for perfect data**? Possibly — missed learning opportunity.
- **Starting with 7 verified companies**? No — this is prudent, not reckless.
- **Launching with bad data**? Yes — brand damage, wasted effort.

**Winner**: Option B again.

## Option Analysis

### Option A: Full Research Then Launch
- **Timeline**: Launch tomorrow (Day 3)
- **Quality**: Highest (60%+ complete data)
- **Momentum**: Lost day of learning
- **Risk**: Lowest data risk, highest time risk

### Option B: Staged Launch (CHOSEN)
- **Timeline**: Launch now (Day 2)
- **Quality**: Preserved (100% on 7 companies)
- **Momentum**: Immediate learning loop
- **Risk**: Contained (7 companies), reversible

### Option C: Hybrid Tier-1 Only
- **Timeline**: Launch afternoon (Day 2)
- **Quality**: Mixed (high on Tier-1, low on Tier-2)
- **Momentum**: Partial learning
- **Risk**: Tier-2 conversion risk

## Why Option B Wins

1. **Immediate learning** — we start the feedback loop today, not tomorrow
2. **Quality preserved** — every outreach uses verified data
3. **Parallel efficiency** — research continues while we execute
4. **Risk contained** — if something breaks, only 7 companies affected
5. **Momentum maintained** — team sees action, not planning paralysis
