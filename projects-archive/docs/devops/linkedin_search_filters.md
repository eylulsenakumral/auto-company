## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "0 results found"
**Solutions:**
1. Expand location radius to 150 miles
2. Remove "company size" filter
3. Use broader job titles ("Safety" instead of "Safety Engineer")
4. Switch to Turkish job titles
5. Target broader industry (Manufacturing instead of Automotive)

### Issue: "Results but poor quality"
**Solutions:**
1. Add company size filter (100-300)
2. Exclude job titles (CEO, Intern, Student)
3. Add keywords ("ISO 45001", "PPE")
4. Focus on specific districts (Nilüfer, Osmangazi)
5. Verify company website existence

### Issue: "Can't find 50 qualified prospects"
**Solutions:**
1. Expand to nearby cities (Izmit, Istanbul - Asia side)
2. Include broader safety roles (Quality Manager, Plant Manager)
3. Lower company size threshold to 50-300 employees
4. Add textile suppliers in Bursa (if automotive pool exhausted)
5. Use Turkish-language searches
