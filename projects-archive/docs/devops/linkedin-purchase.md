## InMail Credit System

### Credit Allocation
- **50 InMail credits/month** included in Core plan
- Credits reset on billing date (not calendar month)
- Unused credits: **Do NOT roll over** - use them or lose them

### InMail vs Connection Request
| Feature | InMail | Connection Request |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Requires connection? | No - message anyone | Yes - must accept first |
| Success rate | Higher (professional outreach) | Lower (easy to ignore) |
| Cost | Uses credits | Free (but limited) |
| Best for | Cold outreach to EHS coordinators | Warm introductions |

**Strategy for NextVision:**
- Use InMail for Bursa automotive company EHS coordinators (cold outreach)
- Reserve connection requests for warm leads or referrals
- Target: 50 InMails/month = 600 outreach opportunities/year

## Risk Assessment

### Low Risk
- **Instant activation** - no downtime waiting for account setup
- **No long-term commitment** with monthly billing
- **Cancel anytime** - effective next billing cycle

### Medium Risk
- **InMail credits expire monthly** - must use 50/month or lose value
- **No refund** for unused partial month if cancelled mid-cycle

### Mitigation
- Start with monthly billing to test ROI
- Track InMail usage weekly to ensure full credit utilization
- Set calendar reminder for monthly credit reset

## ROI Targets for NextVision

### Break-even Analysis
- **Cost:** $119.99/month
- **InMail credits:** 50
- **Cost per InMail:** $2.40
- **Target response rate:** 10% (5 responses/month)
- **Target conversion rate:** 20% of responses (1 qualified lead/month)
- **Target deal size:** Need >$120/customer to break even on first month

### Success Metrics
- **Month 1:** 5-10 positive responses from EHS coordinators
- **Month 2:** 2-3 discovery calls booked
- **Month 3:** 1 closed deal or serious pipeline opportunity

**Decision point:** After 3 months, evaluate ROI. If negative, consider alternative outreach channels.
