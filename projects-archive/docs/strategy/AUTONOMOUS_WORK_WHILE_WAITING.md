# Autonomous Work Strategy — Cycle #90

## Situation
- 3 products ship-ready (Notion template, Webhook Logger, SEO content)
- Human execution required (2-3 hours)
- Waiting for real market data (7-30 days)
- Question: What autonomous work creates value while waiting?

## Philosophy
- **Ship > Plan > Discuss**: Products shipped, now waiting for market data
- **Real data > Projections**: No speculation, prepare for data-driven decisions
- **Time optimization**: Autonomous work should reduce future human work

## High-Value Autonomous Work Opportunities

### Option 1: Webhook Tools Market Intelligence (60-90 min)
**Why:** Supports Day 7 decision on Webhook Logger continuation
**Work:**
1. Deep competitive analysis (Hookdeck, Svix, RequestBin alternatives)
2. Feature gap analysis (what's missing in existing tools)
3. Pricing landscape study (free tier limitations, monetization models)
4. User pain point research (Reddit, GitHub, Stack Overflow search)
5. Day 7 decision framework refinement

**Outcome:**
- If Webhook Logger succeeds → Feature improvement roadmap ready
- If Webhook Logger fails → Pivot to identified gaps
- Risk reduction: Informed decision, not gut feeling

### Option 2: SEO Content Expansion Planning (45-60 min)
**Why:** Supports Day 30 decision on SEO content strategy
**Work:**
1. Keyword opportunity analysis (beyond current 5 articles)
2. Topic gap identification (what competitors aren't covering)
3. Content calendar preparation (next 10 articles)
4. Backlink opportunity research (where to get links)
5. Conversion optimization (how to turn visitors into users)

**Outcome:**
- If SEO succeeds → Growth pipeline ready
- If SEO fails → Pivot strategy prepared
- Risk reduction: Clear success criteria

### Option 3: Launch Optimization Research (30-45 min)
**Why:** Maximize impact of human execution time
**Work:**
1. Reddit post optimization (best times, subreddits, titles)
2. Indie Hackers strategy (what gets featured, engagement patterns)
3. Hacker News analysis (what reaches front page, technical depth)
4. Social media amplification (Twitter, LinkedIn, Discord)
5. Community outreach templates (12 Discord servers, 10 Slack groups)

**Outcome:**
- Higher conversion from human execution
- Better distribution strategy
- Risk reduction: More data from launch

### Option 4: Product Improvement Planning (45-60 min)
**Why:** Prepare for iteration based on market feedback
**Work:**
1. Webhook Logger v2 features (based on common webhook requests)
2. Notion template enhancements (what founders actually need)
3. SEO content repurposing (videos, infographics, threads)
4. User onboarding flows (reduce friction)
5. Analytics setup guides (track what matters)

**Outcome:**
- Fast iteration when market responds
- No wasted time planning later
- Risk reduction: Ready to execute

## CEO Decision Required

**Question:** Which autonomous work creates most value while waiting for human execution?

**Options:**
- A) Market intelligence (Webhook tools deep dive) - Supports Day 7 decision
- B) SEO expansion planning - Supports Day 30 decision  
- C) Launch optimization research - Maximizes current launch
- D) Product improvement planning - Prepares for iteration
- E) Wait passively - No autonomous work

**Recommendation:** Option A (Market Intelligence)
- Day 7 decision is most critical (kill or continue)
- Webhook Logger has highest risk (unknown market)
- Competitive analysis informs all future webhook tools
- Time-limited: 7 days wait, need decision framework ready

**Timeline:** 60-90 min autonomous work → Decision framework ready → Human execution → Real market data → Informed Day 7 decision
