# Product Hunt Launch Checklist: The Complete 30-Day Preparation Guide

Launching on Product Hunt can make or break your startup's early trajectory. A successful launch drives thousands of users, press coverage, and investor interest. But most founders fail because they don't prepare properly.

This guide breaks down the exact 30-day preparation process used by top Product Hunt launches, complete with a downloadable Notion template to track your progress.

## Why Most Product Hunt Launches Fail

After analyzing 100+ launches, we found three common mistakes:

1. **Starting too late** — Beginning preparation 1-2 weeks before launch
2. **No launch day plan** — Wing it on launch day instead of following a schedule
3. **Poor engagement strategy** — Failing to respond to comments and build community

The secret? **Start 30 days in advance** and follow a systematic approach.

## The 30-Day Pre-Launch Checklist

### Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

**Day 1: Define Success Metrics**
- Target: Top 5 Product of the Day
- Goal: 500+ upvotes, 100+ comments
- Set up analytics tracking

**Day 2: Research Competitors**
- Analyze top 10 launches in your category
- Identify differentiation points
- Study their launch assets (hunt posts, comments)

**Day 3: Craft Your Hunt Post**
- Write compelling headline (60 chars max)
- Create clear subtitle (120 chars max)
- Draft first 2 paragraphs (make them count)
- Prepare demo video (60-90 seconds)

**Day 4: Build Your Launch Assets**
- Create gallery images (1200x630px minimum)
- Design social media graphics
- Prepare logo and brand assets

**Day 5: Set Up Landing Page**
- Optimize for Product Hunt traffic
- Add clear CTA (sign up, download, etc.)
- Implement analytics

**Day 6: Build Email List**
- Create pre-launch waitlist
- Prepare launch day email sequence
- Set up email capture

**Day 7: Identify Your Launch Team**
- Recruit 5-10 friends/colleagues as launch supporters
- Coordinate their upvote timing
- Prepare comment engagement

### Week 2: Outreach (Days 8-14)

**Day 8-10: Hunter Outreach**
- Research active hunters in your category
- Build genuine relationships (don't spam)
- Share your product for feedback
- Ask if they'd consider hunting

**Day 11-12: Community Engagement**
- Join relevant communities (Indie Hackers, Hacker News, Reddit)
- Provide value before launch
- Build relationships with key members

**Day 13-14: Social Media Preparation**
- Create social media calendar
- Draft launch day posts
- Prepare responses for common questions

### Week 3: Content & PR (Days 15-21)

**Day 15-17: Content Creation**
- Write blog posts about your product journey
- Create tutorials/use cases
- Record explainer videos

**Day 18-19: Media Outreach**
- Identify tech journalists covering your space
- Craft personalized pitches
- Send press releases 1 week before launch

**Day 20-21: Test Run**
- Practice launch day flow
- Test all systems (analytics, signups)
- Prepare for worst-case scenarios

### Week 4: Final Push (Days 22-30)

**Day 22-25: Countdown Campaign**
- Announce launch date on social media
- Share countdown posts
- Build anticipation

**Day 26-28: Final Preparation**
- Coordinate with hunter (if secured)
- Confirm launch team availability
- Prepare launch day schedule

**Day 29: Rest Day**
- Avoid burnout
- Double-check everything

**Day 30: LAUNCH DAY** 🚀
- 12:01 AM PST: Product goes live
- Respond to every comment within 5 minutes
- Engage with community all day
- Thank supporters publicly
- Monitor metrics real-time

## Launch Day Schedule (Minute-by-Minute)

### 12:01 AM - 3:00 AM (First Hour Critical)
- Product goes live
- Launch team upvotes immediately
- Respond to first 10 comments
- Share on social media

### 3:00 AM - 9:00 AM
- Continue rapid comment responses
- Share in communities
- Monitor upvote velocity

### 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Peak Hours)
- Major engagement push
- Launch team secures top product spot
- Respond to every comment

### 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Maintain engagement
- Share updates/milestones
- Thank community

### 6:00 PM - 11:59 PM
- Final push for comments
- Celebrate with community
- Prepare day 2 follow-up

## Post-Launch Strategy (Days 31-60)

### Day 1 After Launch
- Send email to new users
- Share launch results publicly
- Write "lessons learned" post

### Week 1 After Launch
- Respond to late comments
- Onboard new users
- Fix critical bugs

### Month 1 After Launch
- Analyze metrics and feedback
- Iterate on product
- Plan next marketing push

## Key Success Metrics

Track these metrics throughout:

- **Upvotes**: 500+ for top 5, 1000+ for #1
- **Comments**: 100+ shows engagement
- **Signups**: 10-20% conversion from viewers
- **Traffic**: 5000-10000 visits on launch day
- **Retention**: 30%+ active after 30 days

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ **Not responding to comments** — Engage with every single person
2. ❌ **Launch team upvoting too late** — First hour is critical
3. ❌ **Ignoring negative feedback** — Address concerns publicly
4. ❌ **No CTA on landing page** — Make next step obvious
5. ❌ **Forgetting to say thank you** — Community support matters
